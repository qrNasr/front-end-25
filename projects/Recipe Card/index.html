Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, saut√© the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.

Notes

This recipe serves 2 people. You can add grated cheese on top for extra flavor.

Nutrition Information

Calories: 500 per serving

Protein: 25g

Carbohydrates: 60g

Fat: 15g

Cooking Time

Preparation: 10 minutes

Cooking: 30 minutes

Total: 40 minutes

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.

Storage Instructions

Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.

Variations

You can substitute the minced beef with turkey or a plant-based alternative for a healthier option.

Tips

For a richer flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce while it simmers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I freeze this recipe? Yes, you can freeze the sauce for up to 3 months.

Can I use whole wheat spaghetti? Absolutely! Whole wheat spaghetti is a great alternative.
