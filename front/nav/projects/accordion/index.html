Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and direct bank transfers for specific plans. Please contact support for bank transfer details.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time directly from your account dashboard. Prorated charges or credits will be applied automatically.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Pro plan. No credit card is required to sign up for the trial. You'll get access to all Pro features during this period.

After the trial ends, you can choose to upgrade or your account will automatically revert to the free plan.

How secure is my data?

We take data security very seriously. All connections are encrypted using SSL/TLS. Our infrastructure is hosted in secure data centers, and we perform regular security audits and backups.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new paid subscriptions. If you're not satisfied for any reason within the first 30 days, simply contact our support team for a full refund.
